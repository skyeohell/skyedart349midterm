style guide

This website is a parody of websites for kids tv channels.

color

The colors mimic logos and themes from kids channels like Disney and YTV. The uncovered website will feature spray paint looking text that will be done in a contrasting red.

images

A main chunk of the website will consist of images and graphics for fake kids cartoons that will transform into more explicit imagery. These images will be digitally drawn and will feature cartoony characters with flat colors. On the surface level of the page, the images will be child friendly and very typical cartoon style. The subsurface level of the website will have the same characters depicted in the surface images but in more provocative depictions.

interaction

Interactions will include a button on corner of page to peel away and reveal real website, expanding sections to reveal information (simple image, hover over image to reveal text and expanded information), Tilting text swings up when hovered to read, and scrolling galleries.
